Nature Vancouver - Birding Section#

  • What: Field trips, Birders' Night presentations
  • Find it: naturevancouver.ca/birding

VARC (Vancouver Avian Research Centre)#

  • What: Monthly guided bird walks
  • Vibe: Learn local bird ID with experts
  • Find it: birdvancouver.com/guided-bird-walks

Stanley Park Ecology - Birding with Me#

  • What: Free guided bird walks
  • Vibe: Celebrates diversity in bird and human communities
  • Find it: stanleyparkecology.ca

Greater Vancouver Bird Celebration#

  • What: 2-week series every May
  • Events: Workshops, walks, talks, exhibitions (free!)
  • Find it: destinationvancouver.com

Delta Naturalists Casual Birders (DNCB)#

  • What: Weekly bird walks, mostly weekday mornings
  • Vibe: Friendly, welcoming to newcomers
  • Find it: dncb.wordpress.com

BC Young Birders Program#

  • What: Promotes birding among youth, founded 2014
  • Vibe: Educational, conservation-focused, healthy lifestyles
  • Find it: naturevancouver.ca/birding

Vancouver Birding Group (Facebook)#

  • What: Active online community for local birders
  • Vibe: Share sightings, ask questions, connect with birders
  • Find it: Facebook Group

BC Bird Trail#

  • What: Trail and resources for birding across BC
  • Find it: bcbirdtrail.ca

Birding BC Website#

  • What: Hub for BC birding info, rare bird alerts, forums
  • Vibe: Resource for all skill levels
  • Find it: birding.bc.ca
