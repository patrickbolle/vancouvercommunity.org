Philosophy & Intellectual in Vancouver

9 discussion groups — philosophy circles, Socratic cafes, and deep-conversation meetups for the genuinely curious.

Know a group? Add it Suggest an edit

Vancouver Philosophy Salon #

  • What: Philosophical dialogue and friendly debate. Wide variety of topics, Plato to Buddha
  • Find it: meetup.com/vancouver-philosophy-salon

Shy Philosophers #

  • What: Safe space to discuss philosophy. "Feel safe and converse philosophy with new people freely"
  • Find it: meetup.com/shyphilosophers

Practical Philosophy Club #

  • What: Philosophy discussions, no expertise required
  • When: Weekly in-person meetups
  • Find it: meetup.com/practical-philosophy | practicalphilosophy.club

Mind Matters: A Book Club for Big Ideas #

  • What: Books about big ideas (philosophy, sociology, science). Audiobooks available via VPL, welcoming to all
  • Find it: meetup.com/mind-matters-a-book-club-for-big-ideas

Vancouver Discussions Meetup #

  • What: General intellectual discussions on varied topics. Open-minded, curious participants
  • Find it: meetup.com/vancouver-discussions-meetup-group

The Apeiron Society for the Practice of Philosophy #

  • What: Philosophy practice
  • When: Every 2nd Tuesday (Fall & Winter)
  • Find it: apeironsociety.ca

Anime Philosophy Meetup Vancouver #

  • What: Explore philosophical themes through anime. Unique intersection of pop culture and deep thinking
  • Find it: meetup.com/anime-philosophy-meetup-vancouver

SFU Philosophy Café #

  • What: Public philosophy discussions. Academic but accessible
  • Find it: sfu.ca/philosophy/events

Socrates Café Vancouver #

  • What: Open philosophical inquiry for everyone. Socratic method, no expertise required
  • Find it: meetup.com/topics/socratescafe (check for Vancouver events)
