Vancouver Hack Space (VHS)#

  • What: Volunteer-run workshop, 3D printing, laser cutting, woodworking, robotics, welding
  • Vibe: "Share tools, equipment, space, knowledge, and good times"
  • Find it: vanhack.ca

    • Repair Cafés Vancouver#

  • What: Free community repair events
  • Vibe: Fix things instead of throwing them away, learn repair skills
  • Find it: Various locations, check Eventbrite

    • Vancouver Tool Library#

  • What: Borrow tools instead of buying
  • Vibe: Community resource, sustainable making
  • Find it: Search "Vancouver Tool Library"

    • Venues & Spaces#

    MakerLabs

  • What: 26,000 sq ft — wood shop, metal shop, ceramics, CNC, laser cutters, sewing
  • Find it: makerlabs.com

    • Vancouver Community Laboratory (CoLab)

  • What: Collaborative workshop — wood, metal, textiles
  • Find it: vancommunitylab.com

    • Maker Cube (Langley)

  • What: Woodworking to 3D printing to pottery
  • Find it: makercube.ca

    • Langara Makerspace

  • What: 3D printers, sewing machines, workshops
  • Vibe: Open to everyone, hands-on learning
  • Find it: langara.ca/campus-facilities/makerspace

    • Emily Carr University Maker Labs

  • What: Access to fabrication tools for students and community
  • Vibe: Creative, art-focused making
  • Find it: Emily Carr University
