Mindfulness & Meditation in Vancouver

11 sitting groups across Buddhist, secular, and wellness traditions. Most welcome beginners and meet weekly.

Know a group? Add it Suggest an edit

Mindfulness Practice Community of Vancouver #

  • What: Community meditation, follows Thich Nhat Hanh teachings. Sangha (community) focused
  • Find it: mindfulnessvancouver.org + Meetup

Vancouver Shambhala Centre #

  • What: Buddhist meditation centre with community programs. Warm approach, beginners welcome
  • Find it: vancouver.shambhala.org

Mountain Rain Zen Centre #

  • What: Weekly silent sitting and walking meditation + Buddhist teaching
  • When: Every Wednesday
  • Where: 2016 Wall St, Vancouver
  • Find it: mountainrainzen.org

Kadampa Meditation Centre Vancouver #

  • What: Meditation community for all levels
  • Find it: meditateinvancouver.org

Vancouver Meditation Group (Self-Realization Fellowship) #

  • What: Weekly group meditation
  • Where: 173 West 6th Avenue
  • Find it: vancouvermeditationgroup.org

BC Insight Meditation Society (BCIMS) #

  • What: Buddhist meditation in Theravada tradition. Connects meditation communities across BC
  • Find it: bcims.org

Spiritual Development & Mysticism Meetup #

  • What: Spirit rituals, meditation, mystical experiences. Varied practices, open to exploration
  • Find it: meetup.com/SpiRitual-Artistry

Free Outdoor Meditation (Summer) #

  • What: Drop-in meditation sessions in parks. Accessible, beginner-friendly
  • Find it: vancouver.ca/parks-recreation | Community centre schedules

Inner Engineering Vancouver (Isha Yoga) #

  • What: Free meditation classes, yoga programs, social gatherings. Learn, relax, and have fun
  • Find it: meetup.com/isha-yoga-vancouver-bc

Vancouver Meditation Community (Heartfulness) #

  • What: Group meditation practice downtown
  • Size: 2,000+ members
  • Where: Friend's Quarters, 328 West Hastings St
  • Find it: meetup.com/Vancouver-Heartfulness-Meditation

BE Yoga and Meditation Vancouver #

  • What: Meditation for fulfilled living. Abundance, health, love, inner success
  • Find it: meetup.com/be-yoga-meditation-vancouver

Venues & Resources #

This category is primarily community-focused. Meditation centres function as both venues and communities, with the emphasis on sangha (community practice) rather than just renting space.
