Cycling in Vancouver

12 cycling clubs — social coffee rides, gravel groups, women-and-queer crews, and serious road racing.

Gradient Cycling Club #

  • What: Social rides Wednesdays 6:30pm, Saturdays 8:30am
  • Where: Meet at Pure Bread (5 E. 5th Ave)
  • Find it: Instagram

Lotus Cycling Club #

  • What: Road cycling, group rides, training, workshops
  • Find it: lotuscyclingclub.com

The Last Drop Cycling Club #

  • What: Road bike enthusiasts. "For those who give it everything"
  • Find it: thelastdrop.cc

Vancouver Bicycle Club #

  • What: Touring and recreational cycling since 1976
  • Find it: vbc.bc.ca

Out-n-About Cycling #

  • What: Social cycling for adults. Volunteer-run, exploring Metro Vancouver
  • Find it: meetup.com/out-and-about-cycling-in-metro-vancouver

Vancouver Velo Vets #

  • What: Road cycling, mostly 50+ but all welcome
  • Find it: vancouvervelovets.org

Tri-City Cycling Club #

  • What: Port Moody, Coquitlam, Port Coquitlam area
  • Find it: tccyclingclub.com

Gravel Buddies #

  • What: Gravel riding social group. Women, gender-diverse and 2SLGBTQIA+ focused
  • Find it: gravelbuddies.ca

Gastown Cycling Club #

  • What: Race team and long-distance group rides (75-120km). March to October, varied locations
  • Find it: gastowncycling.com | Instagram

CoffeeOutside YVR #

  • What: Inclusive Friday morning bike group. Community-focused, bring your coffee
  • Find it: Instagram

Kits Social Run Club (also cycling) #

  • What: Social rides in Kitsilano, started 2024. Up-and-coming, friendly community
  • Find it: Instagram

RUNVAN Club #

  • What: Running and cycling community crews. Inclusive, social media connected
  • Find it: runvan.org/crews
