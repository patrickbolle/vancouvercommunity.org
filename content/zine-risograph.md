# 📖 Zine Making / Risograph

## Vancouver Art Book Fair
- **What:** Annual celebration of books as art, zines to monographs. Meet creators, connect with the zine community
- **Find it:** [vancouverartbookfair.com](https://vancouverartbookfair.com/)

## Zine Harvest
- **What:** Semi-annual zine fair at Sun Wah Centre. Meet creators, discover new zines
- **Find it:** [@zine.harvest](https://www.instagram.com/zine.harvest/)

## Venues & Spaces

## Moniker Press
- **What:** Risograph publishing studio. Collaborative, works with artists on books/zines
- **Find it:** [monikerpress.ca](https://www.monikerpress.ca/) / [@monikerpress](https://instagram.com/monikerpress)

## Steph Ford Studio
- **What:** One-woman risograph print studio offering zine-making and riso printing workshops
- **Find it:** [stephfordstudio.com](https://www.stephfordstudio.com/)

## Moniker Press Riso 101 Workshops
- **What:** Online workshops for riso printing beginners. $60 sliding scale, 3-3.5 hours, no prior knowledge needed
- **Find it:** [monikerpress.ca/workshops](https://www.monikerpress.ca/workshops/)

## Lucky's Books and Comics
- **What:** Comics shop that often stocks local zines. Support local zine makers
- **Find it:** [luckys.ca](https://luckys.ca/) | 3128 Main St

## Pulp Fiction Books
- **What:** Independent bookstore with zine section. Curated selection of local and indie publications
- **Find it:** [pulpfictionbooksvancouver.com](http://pulpfictionbooksvancouver.com/) | Main St & Commercial Dr locations
