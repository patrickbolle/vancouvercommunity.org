# 🎤 Poetry / Spoken Word

## Vancouver Poetry House
- **What:** Poetry slams, youth spoken word festivals
- **Find it:** [vancouverpoetryhouse.com](https://www.vancouverpoetryhouse.com/)

## ROLL (Read Out Loud Love)
- **What:** Monthly open mic poetry night. "Rooted in tenderness, community, and shared language"
- **Find it:** [Culture Days](https://culturedays.ca/en/events/405de6f5-fa63-4537-8da2-de6d7a10581c)

## Vancouver Poetry Slam
- **What:** Competitive spoken word community
- **Find it:** [@vancouverpoetryslam](https://instagram.com/vancouverpoetryslam)

## Death Rides a Unicorn
- **What:** Literary and spoken word nonprofit — runs Famous Last Words, Liars of Orpheus, and The 17 Syllable Slam
- **Find it:** [deathridesaunicorn.com](https://deathridesaunicorn.com/)

## Verses Festival of Words
- **What:** Canada's largest annual spoken word festival. Multi-day event, workshops, performances
- **Find it:** [vancouverpoetryhouse.com](https://www.vancouverpoetryhouse.com/)

## WordPlay Poets
- **What:** Outreach program with workshops and performances. Schools and communities across BC
- **Find it:** [vancouverpoetryhouse.com](https://www.vancouverpoetryhouse.com/) (VPH program)

## Carnegie Centre Poetry
- **What:** Downtown Eastside community poetry events. Inclusive, community-focused
- **Find it:** [vancouver.ca/carnegie-community-centre](https://vancouver.ca/parks-recreation-culture/carnegie-community-centre.aspx)
