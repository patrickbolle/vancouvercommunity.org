

## Femme Flip Pinball
- **What:** Women's group.

Meet up to play pinball and make friends. Femme Flip is a gathering for women and non-binary folk to even out the gender gap in competitive pinball. Very welcoming to beginners, all ages, and we meet every second Monday from 7:30 - 9:30pm at The Outpost. We play 5 matches and socialize and hang out in between rounds.
