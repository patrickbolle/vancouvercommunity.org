# Contributing to Vancouver Community Directory

## Our Vision

Vancouver can feel like a hard city to make friends. This directory exists to help people find **real community** — not just things to do, but people to belong with.

## What Belongs Here

Places where you can **find and connect with community** around a shared interest.

### ✅ Include

- Clubs, groups, and collectives you can join
- Meetups with regular gatherings
- Studios, gyms, and spaces with membership communities
- Venues that host niche community events
- Classes where you'd meet like-minded people

### ❌ Exclude

- Generic event listings (concerts, festivals, markets)
- Transactional businesses (shop and leave)
- Resources/directories without community aspect
- Broad "city life" stuff (jobs, housing, buy/sell, food reviews)

### The Test

> *"If I'm interested in X, can I go here and find my people?"*

If yes → it belongs.
If no → it doesn't.

## How to Contribute

1. **Open an issue** describing the group
2. **Submit a PR** editing the relevant category file

Please include:
- Group/event name
- What it is
- Where to find it (website, Instagram, Meetup, etc.)
- Any relevant details (cost, schedule, vibe)
