# 🏓 Pickleball

## Vancouver Pickleball Association
- **What:** Clinics, tournaments, leagues, socials
- **Cost:** $10/year membership
- **Find it:** [vancouverpickleball.ca](https://vancouverpickleball.ca/)

## City of Vancouver Pop-Up Pickleball
- **What:** Temporary conversions of tennis courts
- **Find it:** [vancouver.ca/parks-recreation-culture/pickleball](https://vancouver.ca/parks-recreation-culture/pickleball.aspx)

## YWCA Pickleball
- **What:** Alternate play in group sessions
- **Find it:** [ywcavan.org](https://ywcavan.org/fitness/class-and-training/pickleball)

## North Shore Winter Club Pickleball
- **What:** Socials Monday and Wednesday evenings
- **Find it:** [nswc.ca/programs/pickleball](https://nswc.ca/programs/pickleball/)
