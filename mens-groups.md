# 👔 Men's Groups

## ARKA Brotherhood
- **What:** Leadership training and community for men. Weekly action-focused circles, mature masculinity
- **Find it:** [arkabrotherhood.com](https://arkabrotherhood.com/)

## The DUDES Club
- **What:** Men's talking circles, health and wellness. Participant-led community of brotherhood
- **Find it:** [dudesclub.ca](https://dudesclub.ca/)

## ManKind Project BC
- **What:** Supportive men's talking circles
- **Find it:** [bc.canada.mkp.org/find-a-mens-group](https://bc.canada.mkp.org/find-a-mens-group/)

## Pathways Men's Talking Circle
- **What:** Participant-led community for men's health and wellness. Brotherhood, support circles
- **Find it:** [vancouver.pathwaysbc.ca](https://vancouver.pathwaysbc.ca/programs/3391)

## Metro Vancouver Dads
- **What:** Meetups for fathers with young kids. Casual playground hangouts, parenting support
- **Find it:** [meetup.com/Vancouver-Dads](https://www.meetup.com/Vancouver-Dads/)

## Men's Wellness Groups (Meetup)
- **What:** Fitness, mindfulness, social events for men. Holistic wellness approach
- **Find it:** [meetup.com/topics/mens-health](https://www.meetup.com/topics/mens-health/ca/bc/vancouver/)
