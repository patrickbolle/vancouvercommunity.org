# 🛍️ Flea Markets / Vintage

## The Eastside Flea
- **What:** Seasonal community market, 50+ vendors
- **Vibe:** Vintage clothing, collectibles, plants, handmade
- **Where:** 1739 Venables St
- **Find it:** [eastsideflea.com](http://www.eastsideflea.com/)

## The Granville Flea
- **What:** Pop-up vintage expo
- **Where:** Various locations (Beaumont Studios, etc.)
- **Find it:** [granvilleflea.com](https://www.granvilleflea.com/) / [@granvilleflea](https://instagram.com/granvilleflea)
